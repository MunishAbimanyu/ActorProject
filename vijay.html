Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974),[2][3] known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works mainly in Tamil cinema.[4][5] He is among the highest paid actors in India[6][7] and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list on seven occasions.[8][9] He has played the lead in 67 films. Referred to as Thalapathy (transl. Commander), Vijay has a significant following internationally.[10][6] He has won several awards, including an Osaka Best Actor Award and a South Indian International Movie Award.[11][12] In 2023, he became the highest paid actor in India and one of the top paid actors in the world.[13]
